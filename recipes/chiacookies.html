Chia cookies

Original recipe: Click here

Description

Chia seed cookies are so simple to prepare with fewer ingredients. It is just a combination of whole wheat flour, sugar, and oil. I would not say this cookie is healthy. However, this recipe can be prepared with whole wheat without baking powder or soda. I used dark brown sugar and whole wheat, which turned my cookie brown. The cookie will be light in color if you use white sugar.

Fresh rosemary or dried herbs with olive oil would make an excellent combination in the cookie. If adding herbs, add more salt and avoid vanilla extract. According to Healthline, chia seeds are Despite their tiny size, chia seeds are highly nutritious. They contain fiber, protein, omega-3 fatty acids, and various micronutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Whole wheat flour
  • 1/4+1/8 cups Brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/3 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Â°F
  2. In a bowl combine oil and sugar and mix well until the sugar dissolves in oil.
  3. Add chia seeds, salt, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add whole wheat flour and form a dough.
  5. Shape them into cookies.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes. Once the cookies are cool store in an airtight container.

NOTES: You can add white sugar. If adding white sugar, powder them and add it.
